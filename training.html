TRAINING FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our training programs and services.

Our training programs stand out through:

  • Industry-experienced instructors who are practitioners, not just trainers
  • Customized content tailored to your specific needs and challenges
  • Hands-on, project-based learning approach
  • Focus on practical application and measurable outcomes
  • Continuous support and resources beyond the training sessions

Absolutely! Customization is at the heart of our approach. We begin with a thorough needs assessment to understand:

  • Your business objectives and challenges
  • Current skill levels of participants
  • Specific tools or technologies you use
  • Your preferred learning formats and schedules

We then design a program that precisely addresses your requirements, incorporating your case studies, workflows, and real-world scenarios.

We offer flexible delivery options to suit different learning preferences and logistical needs:

  • In-person training: At your location or our training centers
  • Virtual instructor-led training: Live online sessions with interactive elements
  • Hybrid training: Combination of in-person and online components
  • Self-paced eLearning: Comprehensive online courses with mentor support
  • Blended learning: Mix of different modalities for optimal results

We employ a comprehensive evaluation framework based on the Kirkpatrick model:

  • Level 1 - Reaction: Participant feedback and satisfaction surveys
  • Level 2 - Learning: Pre- and post-assessments to measure knowledge gain
  • Level 3 - Behavior: On-the-job application and behavioral changes
  • Level 4 - Results: Business impact metrics aligned with your objectives

For corporate clients, we provide detailed analytics reports showing ROI and recommendations for continuous improvement.

Yes, we offer several certification options:

  • Optic Bee Certifications: For our proprietary training programs, recognized by industry partners
  • Vendor Certifications: Preparation for AWS, Microsoft, Google, and other technology certifications
  • Custom Corporate Certifications: Designed specifically for your organization's internal standards
  • Digital Badges: Shareable credentials that verify specific skill acquisitions

All certifications require successful completion of assessments and, where applicable, practical projects.
