FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our digital marketing services.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term strategy, and results typically begin to appear within 3-6 months. However, this timeline can vary based on factors like your industry competitiveness, website age, and the current state of your SEO. We often see initial improvements in technical SEO metrics within the first month, with rankings and traffic improvements following in subsequent months.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on improving your website's organic (unpaid) search rankings through technical optimizations, content creation, and link building. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) involves paid advertising where you pay each time someone clicks on your ad. SEO delivers long-term sustainable results, while PPC provides immediate traffic but stops when you stop paying. The most effective strategy often combines both approaches.

How do you measure marketing campaign success?

We measure success through key performance indicators (KPIs) aligned with your business goals. These may include website traffic, conversion rates, lead generation, sales revenue, return on ad spend (ROAS), customer acquisition cost (CAC), and engagement metrics. Before launching any campaign, we establish clear benchmarks and goals to ensure we can accurately measure performance and ROI.

How much should I budget for digital marketing?

Digital marketing budgets vary widely depending on your industry, competition, business size, and goals. As a general guideline, businesses typically allocate 7-12% of total revenue to marketing, with digital marketing representing 40-60% of that budget. During our consultation, we'll analyze your specific needs and recommend a budget that maximizes your return on investment.

Do you offer local SEO services?

Yes, we specialize in local SEO strategies that help businesses improve their visibility in local search results. Our local SEO services include Google My Business optimization, local citations, review management, localized content creation, and geo-targeted campaigns. These strategies are particularly effective for businesses with physical locations or those serving specific geographic areas.
