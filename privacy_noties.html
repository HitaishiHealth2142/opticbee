Privacy Notice

Introduction

Welcome to Optic Bee. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Notice outlines how we collect, store, use, and share your information when you visit our website or use our services. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this notice.

Our platform is designed with privacy and transparency in mind, ensuring users have control over their data. We encourage you to review this notice carefully to understand our data practices. Your continued use of our services signifies your consent to this Privacy Notice. Optic Bee may periodically update this notice to reflect changes in law or our business operations. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Information Collection

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when registering, subscribing to newsletters, or interacting with our content. This includes your name, email address, job title, location, and any preferences you submit. We also collect data automatically using cookies and web beacons, such as your device type, IP address, browser type, and interaction behavior across our platform.

Additionally, we may collect information about how you use our services, including pages visited, time spent on content, and actions taken, to help improve your experience and our offerings. This usage data is often aggregated and anonymized before analysis.

Information may also be collected from third-party platforms you use to log in or link with Optic Bee, such as social media or professional networks. This can include your public profile details and authentication tokens, depending on your privacy settings on those platforms.

Log Information, Cookies and Web Beacons

When you use Optic Bee, our servers automatically record information such as your IP address, browser version, access times, and referring websites. We use cookies to enhance functionality, personalize content, and analyze site traffic. Web beacons are used in emails to track engagement such as open and click rates. You can modify your cookie settings via your browser preferences.

Cookies allow us to remember your preferences, login details, and previously viewed content, ensuring a seamless and efficient browsing experience.

Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser, while persistent cookies remain until they expire or are manually deleted.

We may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners to support advertising and analytics, helping us improve our marketing strategies and understand user demographics.

Web beacons embedded on our site and in emails enable us to measure user behavior and the effectiveness of campaigns without collecting directly identifiable information.

Most browsers provide tools to manage cookies and trackers. By adjusting your settings, you can choose to accept or decline cookies, or to be alerted before one is stored on your device.

Information Use

Your information is used to create and manage user accounts, personalize user experiences, provide access to services, send updates, and improve overall site functionality. We may also use your data for analytics, security, compliance with legal obligations, and customer service support.

Selling of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with service providers, partners, or affiliates that help operate our platform and are contractually obligated to safeguard it under strict confidentiality agreements.

Information Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which we collected it, including satisfying legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

In some cases, we may retain certain information for longer periods to comply with regulatory obligations or resolve disputes. We regularly review our data retention policies to ensure compliance with applicable laws.

If you request deletion of your data, we will take reasonable steps to remove your information from our active systems, except where retention is required by law or legitimate business purposes.

Our commitment to data security means that all retained information is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

Need Help or Have Questions?
If you have any questions about this Privacy Notice or how your data is handled, feel free to contact us.
